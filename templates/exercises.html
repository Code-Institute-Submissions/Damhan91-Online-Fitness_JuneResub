Excercises

This section is to show you what exercises you should be doing on what day. The idea of splitting up of body parts is to allow enough recovery for each body part before returning to work on them again. if you find you are still too sore, consider stretching and using a massage roller to help those sore muscles.

Monday: Arms and Shoulders

Triceps extensions: 3 sets of 8 reps
Biceps curls: 3 sets of 8 reps
Shoulder press: 3 sets of 10 reps
Bench dips: 2 sets of 12 reps
Lateral raises: 3 sets of 10 reps

Wednesday: Legs

Barbell back squats: 3 sets of 8 reps
Dumbbell lunges: 2 sets of 10 reps
Romanian deadlifts: 3 sets of 8 reps
Step-ups: 2 sets of 12 reps
Calve raises: 3 sets of 12 reps

Friday: Back, Chest, and Abs

Bench press: 3 sets of 8 reps
Incline bench press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Chest flyes: 3 sets of 20 reps
Seated Cable rows: 3 sets of 8 reps
Dumbbell bent-over rows: 3 sets of 8 reps
Crunches: 3 sets of 20 reps
